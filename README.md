# Travel Journal
This is a project i worked on for the 4th semester module (web application development) last year. This website creates a comprehensive journaling space curated for people who wants to keep the memories of their travels, in all text, video, audio and image formats inside one digital book.

Just treat it as your partner in crime, capturing memories in all the meaningful content forms to create one complete story, that is your travels, your moments
